Privacy Policy

At Bharat Chronicle, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it and the choices you have.

What We Collect

The only personal information we collect is the email address you voluntarily provide when subscribing to our newsletter. We do not collect names, phone numbers, addresses or any other sensitive personal data. When you browse our site, we may collect anonymised statistics about page views and usage via cookies to improve our content and user experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send periodic newsletters and updates if you have opted in.
  • To respond to questions or feedback you submit through our contact forms.
  • To analyse site traffic and user engagement in aggregate to improve our journalism.

Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade or transfer your email address to outside parties. Your data is stored securely with our newsletter provider, and you can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails. We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information against unauthorised access.

Cookies

Like most websites, we use cookies to understand how visitors interact with our pages. These cookies do not identify you personally; they help us analyse trends and serve content more effectively. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to read our news.

Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the site constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at info@bharatchronicle.one.
